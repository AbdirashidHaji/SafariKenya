About SafariKenya

SafariKenya is a locally-owned travel agency dedicated to showcasing the beauty of Kenya to both domestic and international travelers. Founded in 2010, we have helped thousands of Kenyans explore their own country affordably.

Our team consists of passionate travel experts who have extensive knowledge of Kenya's diverse landscapes, cultures, and wildlife. We believe in sustainable tourism that benefits local communities.
